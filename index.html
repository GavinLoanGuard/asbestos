Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Sample collection typically takes 30 minutes to 2 hours depending on the number of materials being tested. Lab results are usually available within 24-48 hours for standard service, or same-day for rush requests.

Under Alberta's Occupational Health and Safety Code, testing is required before disturbing materials that may contain asbestos in buildings constructed before 1990. This protects both workers and occupants from exposure.

While DIY test kits exist, professional sampling is strongly recommended. Improper sample collection can release dangerous fibers into your home and may produce inaccurate results. Certified professionals use proper safety equipment and techniques.

If asbestos is detected, you'll receive guidance on next steps. Undisturbed asbestos in good condition can often be safely left in place and monitored. If removal is necessary, we can connect you with licensed abatement contractors in Calgary.
